Description

This game was a project assigned at the University of Virginia in the CS 2110: Software Development Methods class. My partners Michael Berrigan, Brandon Blankenship, Kavita Sthanumurthy, and I used the Scrum agile methodology in order to complete this project in a timely and organized manner. Due to a shorter than usual amount of time to complete the project, this meant fulfilling user stories written by ourselves, maintaining a product backlog, and planning for week-long sprints.

The game is a 2D side-scroller that involves killing ghosts with orbs of light from your flashlight. The more ghosts you kill, the more points you get, and the faster they come at you!
